Our Journey

Welcome to ESSA Nyarugunga

Founded in 2005, École Secondaire des Sciences et Administrative (ESSA) Nyarugunga has grown from a small community school to one of the most respected secondary institutions in Kigali's Kicukiro District.

Our school was established with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with character formation. Over the past two decades, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in Rwanda and beyond.

Today, ESSA Nyarugunga stands as a beacon of quality education, offering a diverse range of programs in Technology, Economics, and Hospitality. Our commitment to holistic development ensures that every student leaves not only with knowledge but with values that last a lifetime.
